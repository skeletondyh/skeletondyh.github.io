I’m a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich. I’m fortunate to be advised by Prof. Thomas Hofmann. Currently, I’m interested in understanding and designing neural networks for structured data, as well as their application in scientific discovery.

Previously, I obtained my MPhil from HKUST, advised by Prof. Tong Zhang and Prof. Quanming Yao. Before that, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in computer science from Tsinghua University, where I worked with Prof. Jie Tang.
