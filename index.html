About Me

My name is {{ page.me }}. I'm a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Theo Rekatsinas, and I'm a member of the Systems Group.

My research goal is to understand the fundamental mechanisms of structured intelligence and develop resource-efficient systems to support its application. My topics of interest include but are not limited to: Graph Representation Learning, AutoML, Generative Modeling, and Stochastic Optimization.

Previously, I obtained my MPhil from HKUST, advised by Prof. Quanming Yao and Prof. Tong Zhang. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree in computer science from Tsinghua University, where I worked with Prof. Jie Tang.
